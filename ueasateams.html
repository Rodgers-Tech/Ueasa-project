UEASA Teams

The best part of UEASA is its teams. UEASA was organised to create academic and Problem solving culture for the students(Atuarial science students) at University of Eldoret. UEASA covers all the fields & skills an actuary is required to be an expert in. All teams are headed by the students themselves, who have a proper knowledge about the team they represent.
